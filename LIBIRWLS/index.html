LIBIRWLS

A parallel solver of SVMs and semiparametric SVMs using the IRWLS procedure.

LIBIRWLS is an integrated software support for parallel SVMs and semiparametric SVMs using the IRWLS procedure.

The availability of multi-core processors has motivated an increasing interest in research lines about parallelization of machine learning algorithms. Kernel methods such as Support Vector Machines (SVMs), in spite of their efficacy solving problems of classification, have a very high computational cost and usually produce very large models.

This software is a parallel algorithmic implementation of SVMs and semiparametric SVMs. The goal of the parallelization is to deal with their high training time and semi-parametric solutions are approximations that fix the size of the resulting models in advance and therefore we speed up the classification of new samples.

The package is written in C, and it is released under the MIT license.
